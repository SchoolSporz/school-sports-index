School Sports Index

The 5 Focus Areas: What They Are and Why They Matter

The School Sports Index is built on the Get Kids To Play Audit Framework, an approach refined over two decades of experience by Sportz Village in sports and physical education programs. This framework identifies five critical parameters that directly influence a child's opportunity and experience to engage in active play:

  • Time: Availability of dedicated periods and opportunities for sports and physical activity.
  • Space: Adequate and accessible infrastructure for diverse sports and activities.
  • Child: Measuring children’s participation, satisfaction, and opportunities for competitive exposure.
  • Facilitator: Availability, qualification, and capacity of PE staff and coaches managing programs.
  • Content: Existence of structured, age-appropriate, and inclusive sports curriculum and resources.

These five focus areas help schools assess and benchmark their programs against industry best practices while ensuring a holistic and child-centric sports experience.
